# Insurance

*The group that prices, underwrites, and manages risk transfer across the insurance lifecycle, from policy inception to claim settlement.*

## Actuary

- Build and calibrate pricing models that translate loss experience and exposure data into technical premium rates by product and segment.
- Set reserves for incurred-but-not-reported (IBNR) and outstanding claims, and certify reserve adequacy in periodic reviews.
- Calculate capital requirements (e.g. Solvency II SCR, economic capital) and stress-test the balance sheet against adverse scenarios.
- Produce experience studies on mortality, morbidity, lapse, and persistency to refine assumption sets.
- Sign off on actuarial opinions and embedded value reporting for regulators and senior management.

## Underwriter

- Assess individual risk submissions against appetite guidelines and decide to accept, decline, or apply terms and exclusions.
- Set policy-specific premiums, deductibles, and coverage limits using actuarial rate tables and judgement on non-modelled factors.
- Negotiate terms with brokers and structure facultative placements for large or complex risks.
- Maintain and refine underwriting guidelines, referral thresholds, and authority limits for the book.
- Monitor portfolio accumulation and loss ratios to keep written business within the agreed risk profile.

## Claims Manager / Adjuster

- Triage and investigate incoming claims, verifying coverage, liability, and the legitimacy of reported losses.
- Estimate and reserve individual claim values, adjusting as new information emerges through the claim lifecycle.
- Negotiate settlements with policyholders, third parties, and their representatives within delegated authority.
- Coordinate loss adjusters, surveyors, and legal counsel on contested or high-severity claims.
- Track claims cycle times and leakage, escalating suspected fraud to investigation teams.

## Reinsurance Analyst

- Analyse cession and recovery data to evaluate the performance of treaty and facultative reinsurance arrangements.
- Model retention levels and structure quota-share, excess-of-loss, and stop-loss programmes to optimise risk transfer.
- Prepare submission data and exposure schedules for reinsurer renewals and rate negotiations.
- Calculate reinsurance recoverables and monitor counterparty credit exposure to reinsurers.
- Reconcile ceded premium, commission, and claim balances with reinsurance partners.

## Insurance Product Manager

- Define product propositions, coverage features, and target customer segments across the product portfolio.
- Coordinate filing of policy wordings, rates, and rules with regulators and manage approval timelines.
- Run profitability and competitiveness analysis to decide on product launches, repricing, or withdrawals.
- Align underwriting, actuarial, distribution, and claims teams around a coherent product roadmap.
- Monitor in-force product KPIs such as conversion, retention, and combined ratio to drive iteration.

## Catastrophe / Risk Modeller

- Run vendor and in-house cat models (e.g. RMS, Verisk) to estimate hurricane, earthquake, and flood loss potential.
- Quantify portfolio accumulation and tail metrics such as PML, AAL, and exceedance probability curves.
- Cleanse and geocode exposure data to improve hazard resolution and reduce modelling uncertainty.
- Develop bespoke scenarios for non-modelled and emerging perils such as cyber and climate transition risk.
- Advise underwriting and reinsurance on accumulation limits by region and peril.
