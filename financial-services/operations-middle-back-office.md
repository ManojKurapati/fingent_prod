# Operations (Middle & Back Office)

*The group that processes, confirms, settles, and reconciles transactions so that trades captured at the front office become accurate, settled positions and records.*

## Trade Support / Middle Office Analyst

- Validate and enrich trade captures, resolving booking errors between front office and downstream systems.
- Affirm and confirm trades with counterparties ahead of settlement deadlines.
- Manage trade lifecycle events such as amendments, novations, and cancellations.
- Investigate and resolve trade breaks and economic discrepancies with trading desks.
- Produce intraday position and exposure reports for desk heads and risk.

## Settlements / Clearing Specialist

- Instruct and monitor cash and securities settlement across custodians, CSDs, and clearing houses.
- Manage settlement fails, chase counterparties, and apply buy-in or claims procedures.
- Reconcile nostro and depot balances to ensure delivery-versus-payment integrity.
- Process margin calls and variation/initial margin movements for cleared trades.
- Liaise with clearing brokers and CCPs on give-ups, allocations, and exception handling.

## Reconciliations Analyst

- Run automated and manual reconciliations across cash, positions, and trade records between internal and external books.
- Investigate, categorise, and age reconciliation breaks to root cause.
- Drive break resolution with custodians, counterparties, and internal teams.
- Maintain reconciliation control reports and escalate unresolved or aged items.
- Identify recurring break patterns and recommend process or system fixes.

## Fund Accountant

- Calculate daily and periodic net asset value (NAV) for funds, including pricing and accruals.
- Book income, expenses, fees, and corporate actions into the fund's general ledger.
- Reconcile fund holdings and cash to custodian and administrator records.
- Prepare financial statements and investor reporting in line with the fund's accounting policies.
- Validate NAV against tolerances and resolve pricing exceptions before publication.

## Custody Operations Analyst

- Oversee safekeeping of client assets and maintain accurate holdings records with sub-custodians.
- Process asset transfers, deliveries, and account openings across markets.
- Monitor sub-custodian network performance, fees, and market-specific requirements.
- Handle income collection, tax reclaims, and proxy voting instructions for held assets.
- Reconcile custody positions and resolve asset servicing discrepancies.

## Collateral Management Analyst

- Calculate and issue margin calls under CSA, GMRA, and cleared collateral agreements.
- Agree collateral movements with counterparties and resolve margin disputes.
- Manage eligible collateral, haircuts, and substitutions to optimise funding cost.
- Monitor collateral inventory, concentration, and rehypothecation limits.
- Reconcile collateral balances and pledged assets with counterparties and custodians.

## Corporate Actions Specialist

- Capture and validate corporate action events such as dividends, mergers, splits, and rights issues.
- Notify clients and desks of event terms, deadlines, and election options.
- Process elections, entitlements, and proceeds across mandatory and voluntary events.
- Reconcile expected versus received entitlements and chase outstanding claims.
- Manage tax implications and ex-date/record-date adjustments for affected positions.
