# Product, Strategy & Client

*The group that defines financial products, sets pricing, and owns the commercial relationship across onboarding, service, and distribution.*

## Product Manager (financial products / fintech)

- Own the product roadmap and prioritise features against client needs and commercial value.
- Write requirements and user stories, and coordinate engineering, design, and compliance delivery.
- Define success metrics and analyse adoption, retention, and revenue post-launch.
- Run discovery with clients and stakeholders to validate problems before building.
- Manage the product backlog, release planning, and go-to-market readiness.

## Pricing Analyst

- Build pricing models and fee schedules balancing competitiveness, margin, and risk.
- Analyse price elasticity, win/loss data, and competitor benchmarking.
- Set deal-specific pricing and discount approvals within commercial guidelines.
- Monitor realised margins and recommend repricing where profitability erodes.
- Model the revenue impact of pricing changes before rollout.

## Client Onboarding Specialist

- Guide new clients through account opening, documentation, and system setup.
- Coordinate KYC, legal agreement execution, and entitlements provisioning across teams.
- Track onboarding milestones and remove blockers to shorten time-to-revenue.
- Configure client accounts, connectivity, and standing settlement instructions.
- Hand over fully activated clients to relationship and service teams with complete records.

## Institutional Client Services / Relationship Manager

- Serve as the primary point of contact for institutional clients on queries and escalations.
- Resolve service issues across operations, technology, and product on the client's behalf.
- Conduct service reviews and identify cross-sell and retention opportunities.
- Gather client feedback and channel requirements into product and operations teams.
- Maintain relationship plans, contact mapping, and account health scoring.

## Sales & Distribution Lead

- Own the sales pipeline and revenue targets across direct and intermediated channels.
- Develop distribution partnerships, broker networks, and platform relationships.
- Coordinate go-to-market campaigns and equip the sales team with collateral and training.
- Negotiate and close institutional deals and distribution agreements.
- Forecast sales performance and report pipeline conversion to leadership.
