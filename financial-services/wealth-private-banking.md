# Wealth Management & Private Banking

_The client-stewardship engine that advises individuals and families on growing, protecting, and transferring their wealth across generations._

## Wealth Manager / Financial Advisor
- Develop holistic wealth strategies spanning investments, tax, and retirement
- Recommend and implement portfolios aligned to client goals and risk tolerance
- Conduct regular reviews to rebalance and update plans as circumstances change
- Coordinate with tax advisors, accountants, and estate attorneys for clients
- Educate clients on market conditions, products, and financial decisions
- Grow the book through referrals and deepening existing client relationships

## Private Banker / Relationship Manager
- Serve as the primary point of contact for high-net-worth and UHNW clients
- Provide access to lending, deposit, and bespoke banking solutions
- Arrange Lombard loans, mortgages, and credit against client assets
- Orchestrate specialists across investments, trusts, and family-office services
- Anticipate client liquidity, financing, and lifestyle needs proactively
- Onboard new clients and uphold KYC, suitability, and risk standards

## Investment Counsellor
- Translate the house investment view into client-specific portfolio advice
- Recommend asset allocation and product selection suited to each client
- Present market outlooks and investment opportunities in client meetings
- Bridge portfolio management teams and client-facing relationship managers
- Tailor discretionary and advisory mandates to client objectives
- Monitor client portfolios and proactively suggest tactical adjustments

## Financial Planner
- Build comprehensive financial plans covering cash flow, goals, and protection
- Project retirement funding needs and recommend savings strategies
- Advise on tax-efficient wrappers, pensions, and contribution planning
- Model insurance, education-funding, and major-purchase scenarios
- Update plans through life events such as marriage, inheritance, or sale of business
- Quantify trade-offs between spending, saving, and risk for clients

## Client Portfolio Manager
- Manage discretionary portfolios on behalf of individual wealth clients
- Implement the firm's investment strategy within each client's constraints
- Customise holdings for tax-loss harvesting, concentration, and preferences
- Report performance and positioning clearly to non-professional clients
- Liaise with relationship managers to reflect changes in client circumstances
- Ensure portfolios remain within mandate, suitability, and liquidity guidelines
