# Retail & Commercial Banking

_The everyday-banking engine that serves individuals and businesses with deposits, lending, and credit while managing risk at scale._

## Branch Manager
- Oversee daily branch operations, staffing, and service standards
- Drive deposit, lending, and product sales targets for the branch
- Coach and develop tellers, personal bankers, and front-line staff
- Ensure compliance with cash handling, security, and audit controls
- Resolve escalated customer complaints and retention situations
- Build the branch's presence in the local community and market

## Personal Banker
- Open accounts and onboard retail customers across deposit products
- Cross-sell cards, loans, savings, and digital banking services
- Assess basic credit eligibility for consumer lending products
- Resolve customer servicing requests, disputes, and account issues
- Educate customers on product features, fees, and digital tools
- Identify referral opportunities to mortgage, wealth, and business teams

## Commercial / Business Banking Relationship Manager
- Manage a portfolio of business and corporate banking clients
- Structure working-capital, term, and trade-finance facilities
- Cross-sell treasury, cash-management, and merchant services
- Conduct annual reviews of client financials and credit facilities
- Originate new business clients through networking and referrals
- Coordinate credit, legal, and product partners to deliver solutions

## Credit Analyst (consumer / commercial)
- Analyse borrower financial statements, cash flow, and repayment capacity
- Assign risk ratings and recommend credit limits and pricing
- Prepare credit memos and present cases to credit committees
- Identify and quantify credit, industry, and collateral risks
- Monitor existing exposures for covenant breaches and deterioration
- Recommend conditions, covenants, and structures to mitigate risk

## Loan Officer / Mortgage Advisor
- Guide applicants through loan and mortgage product selection
- Collect documentation and pre-qualify borrowers for financing
- Explain rates, terms, fees, and repayment structures to applicants
- Liaise between applicants and underwriters to progress applications
- Manage the pipeline from application through approval and funding
- Ensure disclosures and regulatory lending requirements are met

## Underwriter (lending)
- Evaluate loan applications against credit policy and risk appetite
- Verify income, employment, assets, and credit history of applicants
- Order and assess appraisals, valuations, and collateral documentation
- Approve, decline, or counter applications with conditions and rationale
- Calculate debt-service, loan-to-value, and affordability ratios
- Document underwriting decisions for audit and secondary-market sale
