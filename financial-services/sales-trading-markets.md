# Sales & Trading / Markets

_The market-facing engine that prices, distributes, and trades financial instruments while managing risk and providing liquidity to clients._

## Trader (equities, fixed income, FX, commodities, derivatives)
- Quote prices and execute client trades while managing the desk's inventory
- Take and manage proprietary or flow positions within assigned risk limits
- Hedge exposures across delta, vega, and rate sensitivities in real time
- Monitor market depth, order flow, and macro catalysts affecting the asset class
- Mark the book accurately at day's end and reconcile P&L attribution
- Adjust pricing and skew dynamically in response to volatility and supply/demand

## Sales (institutional sales, distribution)
- Cover institutional accounts and distribute trade ideas, research, and new issues
- Translate client needs into actionable orders routed to the trading desk
- Communicate axes, inventory, and pricing levels between traders and clients
- Build and maintain long-term relationships with portfolio managers and CIOs
- Negotiate trade terms and ensure best execution for assigned accounts
- Track client wallet share, flows, and feedback to grow the franchise

## Market Maker
- Provide continuous two-way bid/offer quotes to ensure liquidity
- Capture bid-ask spread while managing accumulated inventory risk
- Set quote widths based on volatility, position size, and adverse-selection risk
- Honour exchange or regulatory market-making obligations and quote-presence rules
- Rebalance inventory toward neutral using hedges and offsetting trades
- Monitor for toxic flow and adjust quoting to protect against informed traders

## Quantitative Trader / Quant Researcher
- Design, backtest, and validate systematic trading strategies and signals
- Research statistical edges across price, volume, and alternative datasets
- Calibrate position sizing, risk budgets, and portfolio construction rules
- Monitor live strategy performance versus backtest and flag regime breaks
- Optimise transaction-cost and slippage assumptions in strategy models
- Collaborate with quant developers to productionise validated alpha signals

## Quantitative Developer
- Build low-latency execution, pricing, and risk systems for trading desks
- Implement and optimise quant models into production-grade, tested code
- Maintain market-data pipelines, feed handlers, and tick databases
- Profile and tune system performance to minimise latency and bottlenecks
- Develop backtesting frameworks and simulation infrastructure for researchers
- Manage deployment, monitoring, and failover of trading applications

## Structurer
- Design bespoke derivative and structured products to meet client objectives
- Price complex payoffs using pricing models and embedded optionality
- Document term sheets, payoff mechanics, and risk disclosures for clients
- Decompose structured products into hedgeable underlying components for traders
- Assess regulatory, capital, and accounting treatment of proposed structures
- Reverse-engineer competitor products and propose improved alternatives

## Execution / Algo Trading Specialist
- Select and configure execution algorithms (VWAP, TWAP, implementation shortfall) per order
- Minimise market impact and slippage on large institutional orders
- Route orders intelligently across venues, dark pools, and liquidity sources
- Monitor live executions and intervene when fills deviate from benchmark
- Produce transaction-cost analysis (TCA) reports for clients and desks
- Tune algo parameters based on liquidity conditions and post-trade analytics
