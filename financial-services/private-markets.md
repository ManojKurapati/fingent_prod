# Private Markets

_The illiquid-investing engine that sources, underwrites, and stewards privately held equity, credit, and real-asset investments through their full lifecycle._

## Private Equity Associate / Principal / Partner
- Source, screen, and evaluate buyout and growth-equity investment opportunities
- Build LBO models and run diligence on target financials and operations
- Lead deal execution including structuring, financing, and negotiation
- Sit on or support portfolio-company boards to drive value-creation plans
- Manage exits via sale, IPO, or recapitalisation to maximise returns
- Cultivate relationships with management teams, advisors, and intermediaries

## Venture Capital Investor / Partner
- Source early- and growth-stage startups through networks and inbound flow
- Assess founders, product-market fit, TAM, and competitive moats
- Lead or co-invest in funding rounds and negotiate term sheets
- Support portfolio companies with hiring, follow-on capital, and introductions
- Monitor portfolio metrics and decide on doubling-down or reserve allocation
- Build the firm's brand and thesis within targeted technology sectors

## Private Credit / Direct Lending Analyst
- Underwrite directly originated loans to mid-market and sponsor-backed borrowers
- Model cash flows, leverage, and covenant headroom under downside scenarios
- Structure loan terms, pricing, security, and covenant packages
- Conduct credit diligence on borrowers, sponsors, and industry risk
- Monitor portfolio loans for covenant compliance and early-warning signals
- Prepare investment committee memos with credit ratings and recommendations

## Real Estate Investment Analyst
- Underwrite property acquisitions using cash-flow, cap-rate, and IRR analysis
- Model development, value-add, and stabilised hold scenarios
- Conduct market, tenant, lease, and physical due diligence on assets
- Structure property-level debt and equity financing
- Track local market fundamentals, rents, vacancy, and comparable transactions
- Support asset management, refinancing, and disposition decisions

## Infrastructure Investment Analyst
- Evaluate long-duration assets such as energy, transport, and utilities
- Model concession, availability, and demand-based revenue and cash flows
- Assess regulatory, contractual, and political risk specific to each asset
- Structure project finance debt with long tenors and inflation linkage
- Diligence construction, operating, and counterparty risk on assets
- Monitor portfolio assets against operating and ESG performance targets

## Fund-of-Funds Manager
- Select and allocate capital across underlying GP funds and managers
- Conduct manager due diligence on track record, team, and process
- Construct diversified portfolios across vintage, strategy, and geography
- Negotiate fund commitments, fees, and co-investment rights with GPs
- Manage capital calls, distributions, and cash pacing across commitments
- Monitor underlying fund performance and report to limited partners

## Deal Sourcing / Origination Lead
- Build and manage a proprietary pipeline of investment opportunities
- Cultivate relationships with bankers, brokers, and intermediaries for deal flow
- Screen inbound opportunities against investment criteria and thesis fit
- Develop outbound origination campaigns targeting specific sectors or owners
- Coordinate handoff of qualified deals to deal execution teams
- Track sourcing metrics, conversion rates, and pipeline health
