# Investment Banking

_The advisory and capital-raising engine that helps clients raise capital, execute strategic transactions, and navigate complex financial decisions._

## Investment Banking Analyst / Associate
- Build and maintain three-statement financial models, DCF, LBO, and comparable company analyses
- Draft pitch books, confidential information memoranda (CIMs), and management presentations
- Conduct due diligence by reviewing data rooms, contracts, and financial filings
- Coordinate live deal logistics across legal, accounting, and client workstreams
- Maintain comp sets, precedent transaction databases, and market intelligence trackers
- Prepare board materials and respond to ad-hoc client and senior banker requests

## VP / Director / Managing Director (MD)
- Own primary client relationships and originate new mandates and revenue
- Set deal strategy, valuation positioning, and negotiation tactics for live transactions
- Review and quality-control analyst and associate work before it reaches clients
- Pitch the firm's capabilities to win competitive mandates and beauty parades
- Manage execution risk, fee negotiation, and engagement letter terms
- Mentor junior bankers and allocate staffing across the deal team

## Coverage / Sector Banker
- Maintain deep expertise in a specific industry vertical and its competitive landscape
- Map the universe of clients and prospects within the covered sector
- Generate proactive strategic ideas (M&A, financing, capital structure) tailored to each client
- Track sector M&A activity, valuation trends, and regulatory developments
- Bring in product specialists (M&A, ECM, DCM) to execute on identified opportunities
- Host sector conferences and curate management-team introductions

## M&A Advisory
- Advise buyers and sellers on transaction structure, valuation, and timing
- Run sell-side processes including teaser distribution, bidder management, and auctions
- Perform accretion/dilution and synergy analyses for acquisition scenarios
- Negotiate key deal terms such as purchase price, earnouts, and indemnities alongside counsel
- Manage buy-side target screening and unsolicited approach strategies
- Coordinate antitrust, fairness opinion, and integration-readiness workstreams

## Equity Capital Markets (ECM)
- Structure and price IPOs, follow-ons, rights issues, and block trades
- Advise issuers on equity-story positioning and optimal market windows
- Build the order book and manage investor allocations during bookbuilding
- Coordinate syndicate banks, roadshow logistics, and analyst research timing
- Monitor equity market conditions and investor sentiment to advise on launch readiness
- Manage stabilisation, greenshoe options, and post-issuance lock-up arrangements

## Debt Capital Markets (DCM)
- Structure and price bond issuances, notes, and commercial paper programmes
- Advise issuers on optimal tenor, currency, and coupon structures
- Liaise with rating agencies to position credit narratives and secure ratings
- Run investor calls and bond roadshows to build the order book
- Monitor credit spreads, yield curves, and primary market supply for timing
- Manage syndicate allocation, documentation, and settlement with paying agents

## Leveraged Finance
- Structure and arrange debt financing for buyouts, recapitalisations, and acquisitions
- Build LBO models testing leverage capacity, covenant headroom, and returns
- Underwrite and syndicate term loans, high-yield bonds, and bridge facilities
- Negotiate covenant packages, intercreditor terms, and pricing flex with sponsors
- Assess credit risk across cash-flow stress scenarios and downside cases
- Coordinate distribution to institutional loan and high-yield investors

## Restructuring Advisory
- Advise distressed companies or creditors on balance-sheet repair and turnaround options
- Analyse liquidity, covenant breaches, and runway under stress scenarios
- Design recapitalisation, debt-exchange, and amend-and-extend solutions
- Negotiate with creditor committees, lenders, and bondholders on restructuring terms
- Support in-court (Chapter 11 / administration) and out-of-court processes
- Prepare valuation and feasibility analyses to underpin plans of reorganisation
