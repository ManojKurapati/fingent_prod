# Cross-Cutting Roles (both contexts)

*The group of roles that span insurance and broader finance, supplying the modelling, data, reporting, and delivery backbone that every other function depends on.*

## Financial Modeller

- Build integrated three-statement, forecasting, and valuation models for planning and decisions.
- Structure scenario and sensitivity analyses to test strategic and financing options.
- Model business cases, M&A, and capital allocation with clear assumption documentation.
- Maintain model integrity through version control, audit checks, and error trapping.
- Translate model outputs into decision-ready recommendations for leadership.

## Business Intelligence / Reporting Analyst

- Design and maintain dashboards and management reports across financial and operational KPIs.
- Define metric logic and ensure a single source of truth for reported numbers.
- Automate recurring reporting to cut manual effort and turnaround time.
- Investigate data anomalies and reconcile reported figures with source systems.
- Translate stakeholder questions into self-service analytics and ad hoc analysis.

## Data Engineer / Analytics Engineer (finance domain)

- Build and maintain ETL/ELT pipelines ingesting market, transaction, and reference data.
- Model data warehouses and marts optimised for finance and risk analytics.
- Implement data quality, lineage, and validation controls across pipelines.
- Manage data infrastructure, orchestration, and access governance.
- Partner with analysts and quants to productionise and serve curated datasets.

## ESG / Sustainability Finance Analyst

- Assess portfolio and counterparty ESG ratings, carbon footprint, and climate exposure.
- Build green and sustainable finance frameworks and verify use-of-proceeds compliance.
- Prepare regulatory ESG disclosures (e.g. SFDR, TCFD, CSRD) and investor reporting.
- Integrate climate risk scenarios into financial and risk analysis.
- Screen investments against exclusion policies and sustainability criteria.

## Project / Programme Manager (finance transformation)

- Plan and govern finance and risk transformation programmes across milestones and budgets.
- Manage cross-functional dependencies, RAID logs, and stakeholder alignment.
- Track delivery against scope, time, and cost, escalating risks and changes.
- Coordinate testing, cutover, and change management for system implementations.
- Report programme status and benefits realisation to steering committees.

## Vendor / Outsourcing Manager

- Manage the lifecycle of third-party vendors and outsourcing arrangements from selection to exit.
- Negotiate contracts, SLAs, and pricing, and monitor ongoing service performance.
- Run vendor due diligence and ongoing risk assessment per outsourcing regulation.
- Track vendor obligations, renewals, and concentration risk across the supplier base.
- Coordinate issue escalation and remediation with critical service providers.

## Finance Operations / Shared Services Lead

- Run centralised finance processes such as accounts payable, receivable, and payroll at scale.
- Define service catalogues, SLAs, and KPIs for shared services delivery.
- Drive process standardisation, automation, and continuous improvement across entities.
- Manage the team, capacity planning, and operational controls for the service centre.
- Oversee month-end close coordination and intercompany processing.
