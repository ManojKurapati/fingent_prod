# Finance Systems & Operations

_The technologists and process owners who run the finance platforms, govern financial data, and continuously improve how finance operates._

## Finance Systems / ERP Analyst
- Configure and maintain the ERP and finance application landscape.
- Troubleshoot system issues and manage finance-system support tickets.
- Implement enhancements, upgrades, and new finance modules.
- Manage user access, roles, and segregation-of-duties controls.
- Build integrations between finance systems and source applications.

## Financial Data Analyst
- Build and maintain finance dashboards and reporting datasets.
- Cleanse, validate, and reconcile financial data across sources.
- Develop queries and pipelines to extract financial information.
- Define data definitions and metrics for consistent reporting.
- Surface trends and anomalies through analytical exploration.

## Finance Transformation / Process Improvement Lead
- Identify and prioritise finance process-improvement and automation opportunities.
- Lead transformation projects from design through implementation.
- Map current and future-state finance processes.
- Deploy automation and AI tooling to eliminate manual work.
- Measure efficiency gains and drive change adoption across teams.

## Order-to-Cash / Procure-to-Pay Process Owner
- Own the end-to-end O2C or P2P process design and performance.
- Define standard procedures and controls across the process lifecycle.
- Monitor process KPIs such as cycle time, accuracy, and exceptions.
- Resolve cross-functional process breakdowns and bottlenecks.
- Govern process changes and ensure consistency across systems and teams.
