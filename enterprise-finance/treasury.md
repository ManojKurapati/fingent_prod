# Treasury

_The guardians of cash, liquidity, and financial risk who ensure the organisation can always meet its obligations and fund its growth._

## Treasurer
- Set treasury policy covering liquidity, investment, and risk management.
- Own banking relationships and credit-facility negotiations.
- Manage the company's capital and debt structure execution.
- Oversee enterprise financial-risk exposures and hedging strategy.
- Report cash and liquidity position to the CFO and board.

## Assistant Treasurer
- Run daily treasury operations and execute the Treasurer's policies.
- Manage bank account structures, signatories, and connectivity.
- Oversee debt compliance, covenants, and interest payments.
- Deputise for the Treasurer on funding and banking matters.
- Supervise treasury analysts and operational controls.

## Treasury Analyst / Manager
- Execute cash positioning, transfers, and short-term investments.
- Prepare treasury reports on cash, debt, and bank balances.
- Monitor covenant compliance and prepare lender reporting.
- Administer treasury management systems and bank portals.
- Support debt issuances, refinancings, and rating processes.

## Cash Management Analyst
- Produce the daily cash position across all bank accounts.
- Execute payments, sweeps, and pooling arrangements.
- Reconcile bank statements to internal cash records.
- Optimise bank fees and account structures.
- Maintain controls over payment initiation and approval.

## Liquidity / Working Capital Analyst
- Build short- and medium-term cash-flow forecasts.
- Analyse working-capital drivers across AR, AP, and inventory.
- Recommend actions to free trapped cash and improve the cash-conversion cycle.
- Model liquidity stress scenarios and minimum-cash buffers.
- Track liquidity KPIs and report against targets.

## FX & Hedging Analyst
- Identify and quantify foreign-currency exposures across the business.
- Execute FX trades and hedging instruments per policy.
- Value derivative positions and monitor hedge effectiveness.
- Track interest-rate and commodity exposures for hedging.
- Prepare hedge accounting documentation and mark-to-market reporting.
