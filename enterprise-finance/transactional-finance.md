# Transactional / Operational Finance

_The high-volume engine room that processes payments, billings, collections, and payroll to keep cash and operations flowing._

## Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk / Manager
- Process supplier invoices and match them to purchase orders and receipts.
- Schedule and execute vendor payment runs within agreed terms.
- Reconcile supplier statements and resolve payment discrepancies.
- Manage the AP aging and prevent late-payment penalties.
- Maintain vendor master data and payment controls against fraud.

## Accounts Receivable (AR) Clerk / Manager
- Record customer payments and apply cash to open invoices.
- Reconcile the AR sub-ledger and investigate unapplied receipts.
- Monitor the receivables aging and report on DSO.
- Resolve customer billing queries and short payments.
- Maintain customer master data and credit limits.

## Billing Specialist
- Generate accurate customer invoices from contracts and usage data.
- Apply correct pricing, discounts, and tax to each invoice.
- Process credit notes, adjustments, and rebills.
- Validate billing data against orders before release.
- Resolve invoice disputes related to amounts or formatting.

## Collections / Credit Control Analyst
- Chase overdue accounts through reminders, calls, and escalations.
- Assess customer creditworthiness and set credit limits.
- Negotiate payment plans for delinquent accounts.
- Forecast cash collections and flag at-risk balances.
- Recommend accounts for write-off or referral to agencies.

## Payroll Specialist / Manager
- Process accurate, on-time payroll across all pay cycles.
- Calculate wages, overtime, bonuses, and statutory deductions.
- Remit payroll taxes and benefit contributions to authorities.
- Reconcile payroll accounts and resolve pay discrepancies.
- Maintain employee payroll records and compliance documentation.

## Procurement / Purchasing Analyst
- Raise and manage purchase orders against approved requisitions.
- Source suppliers and negotiate pricing and terms.
- Track purchasing spend against budget and category targets.
- Maintain supplier contracts and catalogue pricing.
- Analyse spend data to identify savings and consolidation opportunities.
