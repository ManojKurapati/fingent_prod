# Tax

_The specialists who manage the organisation's tax obligations, optimise its tax position, and ensure compliance across every jurisdiction._

## Head of Tax / VP Tax
- Set global tax strategy and the group's effective-tax-rate objectives.
- Manage tax risk, audits, and disputes with revenue authorities.
- Advise leadership on the tax impact of major transactions and structures.
- Oversee the tax provision and tax disclosures in financial statements.
- Lead the tax team and external tax advisor relationships.

## Tax Manager
- Manage preparation and filing of corporate income-tax returns.
- Review tax provisions and reconcile current and deferred tax.
- Coordinate responses to tax authority queries and audits.
- Monitor legislative changes and assess their impact.
- Supervise tax accountants and review their workpapers.

## Tax Accountant / Analyst
- Prepare tax computations and supporting schedules.
- Compile data for income-tax and information returns.
- Reconcile tax accounts in the general ledger.
- Track filing deadlines and payment obligations.
- Maintain documentation supporting tax positions.

## Transfer Pricing Specialist
- Set and document intercompany pricing policies across entities.
- Prepare transfer-pricing documentation and master/local files.
- Benchmark intercompany transactions against arm's-length data.
- Defend transfer-pricing positions in audits and disputes.
- Model the tax impact of supply-chain and entity restructuring.

## Indirect Tax (VAT/Sales Tax) Specialist
- Prepare and file VAT, GST, and sales-tax returns by jurisdiction.
- Determine correct indirect-tax treatment of transactions.
- Reconcile indirect-tax accounts and recover input credits.
- Configure tax determination logic in billing and ERP systems.
- Manage indirect-tax registrations and audit responses.

## International Tax Specialist
- Advise on cross-border structuring and permanent-establishment risk.
- Analyse withholding-tax obligations and treaty relief.
- Manage foreign tax credits and repatriation planning.
- Assess BEPS, Pillar Two, and CFC implications.
- Coordinate compliance across multiple foreign jurisdictions.
