# Corporate Development & Strategy

_The deal-makers and strategists who drive inorganic growth, evaluate transactions, and shape the company's relationship with investors._

## Head of Corporate Development
- Define the M&A and corporate-development strategy aligned to corporate goals.
- Build and prioritise the pipeline of acquisition and partnership targets.
- Lead negotiations and transaction execution through to close.
- Present deal rationale and returns to the board and executives.
- Oversee post-merger integration planning and value capture.

## M&A Analyst / Manager
- Build acquisition valuation and accretion/dilution models.
- Conduct financial and commercial due diligence on targets.
- Prepare deal materials, investment memos, and board decks.
- Coordinate advisors, legal counsel, and diligence workstreams.
- Track deal economics and synergy assumptions post-close.

## Corporate Strategy Analyst
- Analyse markets, competitors, and industry trends for strategic planning.
- Build the analytical case for entry, exit, and growth decisions.
- Model long-term strategic scenarios and their financial outcomes.
- Support the development of multi-year corporate strategy.
- Prepare strategy presentations for executive and board review.

## Investor Relations (IR) Officer / Manager
- Manage communications with shareholders, analysts, and the investment community.
- Prepare earnings releases, investor presentations, and Q&A scripts.
- Gather analyst and market feedback for management.
- Coordinate earnings calls, roadshows, and investor meetings.
- Monitor consensus estimates, ownership, and share-price drivers.
