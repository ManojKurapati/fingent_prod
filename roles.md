# Finance Roles

A comprehensive map of roles across two contexts:
- **Enterprise Finance** — the finance function *inside* a company (the buyer/operator of finance).
- **Financial Services** — organisations whose *product* is finance (banks, asset managers, insurers, fintech).

Use this as the role catalogue for an AI-native finance organisation — each role is a candidate to augment or agentify.
